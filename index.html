Introduction

The dataset is from IS 507 courses.Our professor get data from the Food and Agriculture Organization (FAO), specifically from the FAOSTAT group. The data shows the value of Soybean Export to Africa from 1961 to 2018.I choose line plot in Vega-lite since line plot can show the change of value better. I use component Float to show two pictures below the introduction which can make my blog attractive.
